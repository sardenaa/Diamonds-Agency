# 👥 Human Interaction Protocol

High-hospitality luxury services require a balance between digital efficiency and personal human attention. Below is the comprehensive operational guide detailing every touchpoint in the Meryet Amen Sovereignty (MAS) system that requires active human interaction, explaining **How**, **When**, and **Why** these steps must be executed.

---

## 🏛️ Administrative Operations

### 1. Booking Dispatch & Staff Assignments
* **When**: As soon as a client schedules a new luxury expedition.
* **Why**: High-net-worth travelers expect bespoke personnel. Automated placeholders are initially assigned, but a human concierge must coordinate physical availability and verify credentials.
* **How**:
  1. Open the **Admin Console** and navigate to the **Active Bookings Ledger**.
  2. Locate any booking with pending assignments (marked with `Assigning Luxury Driver...` or `Assigning Personal Egyptologist...`).
  3. Select and assign a verified, certified Egyptologist and a dedicated professional chauffeur from the available staff.
  4. Confirm vehicle readiness (e.g., Mercedes-Benz S-Class / Maybach fleets) and hotel dispatch times.

### 2. Live Support & Ticket Resolution (Gemini AI Copilot)
* **When**: Whenever a guest raises an issue, submits a special request, or requests a dietary modification through their 24/7 Butler Support interface.
* **Why**: A purely robotic response damages client trust. While Gemini AI generates optimized, high-hospitality drafts instantly, an administrative agent must review, customize, and finalize the tone to ensure genuine luxury care.
* **How**:
  1. Navigate to the **Support Ticketing Room** in the Admin Dashboard.
  2. Click on a pending ticket to view the conversation log and the system-generated Gemini AI reply draft.
  3. Review the draft to ensure the response respects VIP protocols, customizes options to the guest's hotel/room number, and answers all specific queries.
  4. Manually edit or enrich the message as needed, then click **Submit Dispatch** to send.

### 3. Google Sheets Synchronization
* **When**: Daily or weekly during financial and itinerary reconciliation.
* **Why**: To share a live spreadsheet ledger of all bookings with accounting, transport departments, and external ground operations teams without exposing the server environment.
* **How**:
  1. Open the **Google Sheets Sync** tab in the Admin Dashboard.
  2. Click **Sign in with Google** to authorize OAuth permissions to write to your designated corporate Drive.
  3. Once connected, click **Export Ledger to Sheet** to compile and push live booking states, transaction weights, dates, and client information.

### 4. Custom Marketing Campaign Broadcasts
* **When**: Prior to seasonal launches, holiday weekends, or VIP upgrades opportunities.
* **Why**: Tailoring promotional outreach to client segments (e.g., high-spending clients vs. cultural enthusiasts) improves conversion and respects client boundaries.
* **How**:
  1. Go to the **WhatsApp Broadcast Engine** in the CRM dashboard.
  2. Filter clients by tier (e.g., Platinum, Gold) or interests.
  3. Choose a template (e.g., *Summer Red Sea Soirée*, *Heli-Tour Upgrade*) or compose a custom text.
  4. Manually audit the estimated queue list to prevent accidental spamming of key clients, then click **Trigger Broadcast**.

## 👑 Guest Touchpoints

### 1. Digital Signature Execution (Agreement Seal)
* **When**: During the tour checkout process.
* **Why**: The Luxury Service Agreement holds legal binding obligations. A handwritten, touch-drawn signature guarantees that the guest has explicitly authorized the terms of service, custom itinerary pricing, and cancellation covenants.
* **How**:
  1. Complete the booking details in the check-out window.
  2. Scroll down to the **VIP Luxury Agreement** signature pane.
  3. Using a stylus or finger on a touchscreen (or mouse cursor on desktop), draw the signature inside the canvas.
  4. Click **Confirm Signature** to embed the vector graphic directly into the downloadable agreement and boarding pass PDFs.

### 2. Pre-Travel Packing Assistant Checklists
* **When**: 1 to 5 days before the scheduled departure.
* **Why**: Insufficient preparation can disrupt a luxury desert or marine cruise. Guests use this private utility to ensure critical documents and specialized expedition outfits are ready.
* **How**:
  1. Open the personal **VIP Guest Ticket Portal**.
  2. Click **Luxury Packing Assistant** to view a tailored pre-travel checklist automatically generated based on the tour environment (Desert, Nile Cruise, or Red Sea Yachting).
  3. Check off items (e.g., *Passport & Copy of Entry Visa*, *Polarized anti-glare marine sunglasses*, *Premium Breathable Linen Apparel*) as they are packed.
  4. Type in any personal specialty items (e.g., prescription medications, specific camera lenses) in the input bar and click **Add Item** to save them locally.

### 3. Custom Pickup Venue Specification
* **When**: During checkout of a tailored royal tour.
* **Why**: High-net-worth clients frequently stay at private luxury estates, residential yachts, or off-grid palaces not listed in default curated lists. 
* **How**:
  1. On the booking checkout sheet, open the **Pickup Hotel Venue** selector.
  2. Choose a curated premier hotel partner from the dropdown list, or choose the **Other / Custom Hotel** option.
  3. Enter the specific custom palace, residential address, or helipad coordinates in the input box to alert the VIP transport dispatch.
