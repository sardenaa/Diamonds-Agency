# Sovereign Administrative Gateway Credentials & System Architecture Guide

Welcome to the **MAS Agency Sovereign System**. This document outlines all credentials, roles, PIN configurations, and interface access routes to securely manage luxury expeditions, clientele communications, content catalogs, and automated campaigns.

---

## 🔒 1. Accessing the Admin Console

The Admin Console is fully integrated into the main application. You can access it using any of the following intuitive routes:

1. **Header Switcher (Desktop & Mobile):**
   * On **Desktop**, look at the top-right switcher rail. Alongside **Tours** and **My Bookings**, you will see **Admin Console 🔒** (or **لوحة التحكم / الإدارة 🔒** in Arabic).
   * On **Mobile**, use the role buttons under the header to toggle **Admin 🔒** directly.
2. **Staff Portal (Footer Link):**
   * Scroll down to the global footer page margin and click the **Staff Portal** (`بوابة الموظفين`) link on the bottom-right.
3. **Clearance Gate Entry:**
   * Selecting any of these options immediately transfers you to the **Sovereign Access Clearance Gate** (`بوابة التحقق الأمني السيادي`) to authenticate.

## 🔑 2. Security Clearance Tiers & Credentials

Authentication is managed via tactical keypad PIN verification. There are three levels of security clearance:

| Clearance Level | Role Title | Access PIN | Responsibilities & Powers |
| :--- | :--- | :---: | :--- |
| **Level 3** | **Sovereign Admin** | `8899` | **Master Access:** Full control over sales analytics, CMS catalog, CRM profiles, support tickets, promo codes, blog editing, live Google Sheets sync, and master audit logs. |
| **Level 2** | **Operations Manager** | `4455` | **Logistics Scheduling:** Assign private expert scholar guides (e.g., Dr. Salim Hassan) and luxury Mercedes-Benz executive chauffeurs. Manage active promo coupons. |
| **Level 1** | **Guest Relations Coordinator** | `2233` | **Client Relations:** Access client CRM details, dispatch localized automated email/support butler chats, manage WhatsApp blasts, and handle support ticketing. |

## 📊 3. Core Admin Dashboard Modules

Once authorized at the Clearance Gate, the interface unlocks tailored panels depending on your role tier:

### 📈 Sales & Revenue Analytics
* **Real-time Metrics:** Monitor total revenue, estimated profits, booking counts, and average customer transaction values.
* **Granular Visual Charts:** Compare sales performance by tour category, guests' nationality distributions, chauffeur assignments, and scholar-guide engagement metrics.

### 🚗 Operations & Fleet Scheduling
* **Active Ledger:** Track departure dates, hotel pickup details (room numbers), special VIP requests, and payment statuses.
* **Resource Assignment:** Update reservation states (e.g., *Sovereign Confirmed*, *Completed*), assign expert Egyptologists, or dispatch dedicated V-Class chauffeurs.
* **Instant Refund Authorization:** Issue refunds to automatically update guest records, with complete secure logging.

### 👥 VIP CRM Database & Bulk Campaigns
* **Client Dossiers:** Add, edit, search, or delete persistent profiles of premium travellers.
* **Manual Communications:** Dispatch verified WhatsApp notifications or publish Support Butler chats.
* **WhatsApp Bulk Promotion Blast:** Filter customer segments (e.g., *VIP*, *Historical Enthusiasts*, *All*) and send bulk promotional template alerts instantly.

### 🎫 Support Ticketing Desk
* **Priority Inbox:** Manage incoming customer requests, sorted from *Low* to *Royal* priority.
* **AI Copilot (Gemini Integration):** Click **Draft AI Reply** to let Gemini review the user's booking history and draft custom high-end replies instantly.

### 📝 Content Management System (CMS)
* **Luxury Excursions Catalog:** Create, edit, and delete tours, update prices, duration, capacities, and custom itineraries.
* **Blog Articles Editor:** Publish dual-language (Arabic & English) articles with custom cover illustrations and categories.
* **Promo Coupons Generator:** Create custom validation coupons with percentage discounts and automatic expiration guards.

### 🛡️ System Audit Logs
* **Security Telemetry:** Monitor actions such as logins, CSV bookkeeping exports, refunds, and broadcast campaigns with precise IP tracking.

### 🟢 Google Sheets Synchronization
* **Sovereign Bookings Ledger:** Securely authorize via Google OAuth and synchronize live booking details directly into a shared spreadsheet.
